Achievements

Achievements are fun goals for you to complete throughout
each game, ones you have completed will appear green

Master Guesser

Win guessing game with the range set to at least 10,000 and in 15 or under tries

Sweeper or Sweeped?

Win or lose with a reverse sweep in Rock Paper Sciccors

Tic Tac Toe Loser

Somehow lose to the Tic Tac Toe AI (very bad)

2048 Ambassador

Reach a score of 3000 or higher in 2048

Grand-master Guesser

Win guessing game with the range set to at least 1 million and in 20 or under tries

2048 Comple-tionist

Combine two 1024's to reach 2048
